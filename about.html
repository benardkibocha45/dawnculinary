Our Journey

Welcome to Dawn Culinary

Born from a passion for creating memorable dining experiences, Dawn Culinary Sunrise opened its doors in 2018 with a simple mission: to blend comfort with exceptional flavor in Nairobi's vibrant culinary scene.

Our founder, Chef Wanjiru, envisioned a space where every meal feels like a celebration - whether it's a sunrise breakfast meeting or a sunset cocktail with friends. Today, we continue this tradition with seasonal menus that showcase Kenya's finest ingredients through creative, globally-inspired preparations.
