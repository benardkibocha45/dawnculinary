Welcome To

Dawn Culinary Sunrise

Every sunrise brings new possibilities, and at Dawn Culinary, we celebrate this through food that comforts, excites, and brings people together.

Founded on the belief that great meals create lasting memories, our restaurant blends contemporary techniques with the vibrant flavors of Kenya. Whether it's our signature breakfast bowls that start your day right or our sunset cocktails that toast to life's moments, we're here to make every visit special.
